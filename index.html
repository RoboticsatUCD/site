Welcome to the home page of the Robotics Club

We're the Robotics Club! There's always a project going on, whether it be for competition, for research, or for fun. If you want to get involved in robotics, join one of our teams or head up a project of your own, and we'll help you put together a team. Project headquarters is Physics Trailer 1. Here it is on Google Maps, and here is someone walking to it. You can also walk to it by going around instead of going through the Physics building.

The Davis Robotics Club wishes to provide students with an opportunity to supplement their education with hands-on experience in integration engineering. This club will allow students to work together with students of different majors, and to be members of a team that integrates mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science.

The club is structured more as a self motivated workshop, where bigger projects are headed by student leaders rather than "officers." This means that people are working more on what they actually want to work on, rather than the club telling members what to do.

No announcements will be posted on this page, since we do not have a dedicated web-master. If you would like to receive updates, please join our club on OrgSync or Facebook.

Feel free to check out some of the projects that we're working on, although our GitHub account is going to be more up to date than those pages.
